Who am I?

Hello, my name is India and I am Full Stack Web Developer. Born in Detroit, Michagan but raised and educated in Moriarty, New Mexico, at a young age I had a passion for technology. In early 2004, at ten years old, I set up a successful ebay store for my mother. This was my first introduction to the internet and online sales. Starting with old Gameboys I grew into consoles, then phones before getting my own computer at fifteen. This allowed me to apply for jobs in Albuquerque and eventually become independent. From there I took a job as technical support for Canon in their printer and copier department and I realized my passion for the technology field. Then I was employed with Fidelity in their customer support division before becoming technical support once again at Unity BPO in their medical equipment department. Eventually I was promoted to a technical support trainer and instructor at Lavu. I hope to continue my education and grow my experience as I more deeply explore this field.
